Scleroderma

Overview

Scleroderma is a group of autoimmune diseases that cause hardening and tightening of the skin and connective tissues.

Causes

  • Overproduction of collagen
  • Autoimmune response
  • Genetic and environmental factors

Symptoms

  • Thickened, tight skin
  • Joint stiffness
  • Digestive issues

Treatment

No cure; treatments include immunosuppressants, physical therapy, and symptom management.

Prevention

No known prevention, but early diagnosis and treatment can reduce complications.
