Overactive Bladder

Overview

Overactive Bladder is a condition that causes a sudden urge to urinate, often leading to frequent urination and possible leakage.

Causes

It is caused by neurological disorders, diabetes, bladder abnormalities, or weak pelvic muscles.

Symptoms

  • Sudden urge to urinate
  • Frequent urination (day and night)
  • Urge incontinence (leakage)
  • Sleep disturbance due to nighttime urination

Treatment

Treatment includes bladder training, pelvic floor exercises, medications, and nerve stimulation therapy.

Prevention

Improved by healthy lifestyle, weight management, and avoiding bladder irritants.
