Vascular Sarcoma

Overview

A rare and aggressive cancer that forms in the cells lining blood vessels or lymphatic vessels.

Causes

  • Exact cause unknown.
  • Radiation exposure or chronic lymphedema may increase risk.

Symptoms

  • Painful or enlarging mass.
  • Skin discoloration over affected area.
  • Swelling and bleeding in tumors.

Treatment

  • Surgical removal of tumor.
  • Radiation and chemotherapy.
  • Targeted therapy in some cases.

Prevention

  • Reduce unnecessary radiation exposure.
  • Monitor chronic swelling conditions.
