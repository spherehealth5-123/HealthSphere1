Urethritis

Overview

Urethritis is inflammation of the urethra, the tube that carries urine from the bladder to outside the body.

Causes

  • Sexually transmitted infections (gonorrhea, chlamydia)
  • Bacterial infections
  • Chemical irritants

Symptoms

  • Painful urination
  • Itching or irritation
  • Discharge from urethra

Treatment

Antibiotics or antivirals depending on the cause, pain relief medication.

Prevention

Safe sexual practices, good hygiene, and avoiding irritants.
