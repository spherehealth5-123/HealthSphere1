VACTERL Association

Overview

A rare condition characterized by the presence of birth defects affecting multiple body systems, including vertebrae, heart, limbs, and digestive tract.

Causes

  • Exact cause unknown.
  • Believed to be due to genetic and environmental factors during early development.

Symptoms

  • Vertebral defects
  • Heart abnormalities
  • Tracheoesophageal fistula
  • Limb malformations

Treatment

  • Surgical correction of structural problems.
  • Ongoing medical management for heart and digestive issues.

Prevention

  • No known prevention.
  • Early detection and supportive care improve outcomes.
