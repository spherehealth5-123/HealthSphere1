Pancreatic Cancer

Overview

Pancreatic cancer begins in the tissues of the pancreas and is often detected in advanced stages.

Causes

  • Genetic mutations
  • Chronic pancreatitis
  • Smoking and alcohol use

Symptoms

  • Abdominal pain
  • Weight loss
  • Jaundice
  • Loss of appetite

Treatment

  • Surgery (Whipple procedure)
  • Chemotherapy
  • Radiation therapy

Prevention

  • Healthy lifestyle and diet
  • Avoid smoking and alcohol
  • Genetic testing for high-risk individuals
