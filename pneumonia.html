Pneumonia

Overview

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs. It can cause cough, fever, and difficulty breathing.

Causes

  • Bacterial infection (Streptococcus pneumoniae)
  • Viral infections like influenza
  • Fungal infections in weakened immune systems

Symptoms

  • Chest pain when breathing
  • Cough with phlegm
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fever and chills

Treatment

  • Antibiotics for bacterial pneumonia
  • Antiviral medicines if caused by viruses
  • Hospitalization in severe cases

Prevention

  • Vaccination (pneumococcal vaccine)
  • Good hygiene and handwashing
  • Quit smoking
