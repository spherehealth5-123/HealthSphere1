Ependymoma

Overview

Ependymoma is a rare tumor of the brain or spinal cord that develops from ependymal cells lining the ventricles.

Causes

Exact cause unknown; linked to genetic mutations and family history of brain tumors.

Symptoms

  • Headaches
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Seizures
  • Balance and coordination problems

Treatment

Surgery, radiation therapy, and chemotherapy for aggressive cases.

Prevention

No known prevention; early detection improves outcomes.
