Zinc Deficiency

Overview

Zinc deficiency occurs when the body does not get enough zinc, a vital trace mineral for immune function, growth, and wound healing.

Causes

  • Poor dietary intake
  • Malabsorption disorders (celiac, Crohn's disease)
  • Chronic illnesses or excessive alcohol use

Symptoms

  • Hair loss
  • Delayed wound healing
  • Loss of appetite
  • Frequent infections
  • Growth retardation in children

Treatment

  • Zinc supplementation orally
  • Dietary increase in zinc-rich foods (meat, legumes, nuts)
  • Treat underlying absorption issues if present

Prevention

  • Balanced diet with adequate zinc
  • Monitoring at-risk populations (vegetarians, malnourished children)
