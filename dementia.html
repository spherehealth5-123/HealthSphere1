Dementia

Overview

Dementia is a group of disorders that affect memory, thinking, and social abilities, often seen in older adults.

Causes

Caused by damage to brain cells; Alzheimer’s disease is the most common cause.

Symptoms

  • Memory loss
  • Difficulty communicating
  • Poor judgment
  • Confusion and disorientation

Treatment

No cure; treatment includes medications, cognitive therapy, and supportive care.

Prevention

Healthy lifestyle, brain exercises, managing blood pressure and diabetes.
