Tarlov Cysts

Overview

Tarlov cysts are fluid-filled sacs that form around spinal nerve roots, often in the sacral region of the spine.

Causes

  • Congenital weakness in nerve sheath
  • Trauma or spinal injury

Symptoms

  • Lower back pain
  • Numbness or tingling
  • Bladder or bowel dysfunction (in severe cases)

Treatment

Observation if asymptomatic, pain management, surgical drainage, or removal if severe.

Prevention

No prevention; early detection helps in managing symptoms effectively.
