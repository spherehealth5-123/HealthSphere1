Fanconi Anemia

Overview

Fanconi anemia is a rare inherited blood disorder that affects bone marrow, reducing the production of all types of blood cells.

Causes

Mutations in genes responsible for DNA repair.

Symptoms

  • Fatigue
  • Frequent infections
  • Easy bruising
  • Skeletal abnormalities

Treatment

Bone marrow transplant, hormone therapy, and supportive care.

Prevention

Genetic counseling and carrier screening in families with history of the disease.
