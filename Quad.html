Quadriplegia

Overview

Quadriplegia is paralysis of all four limbs, usually caused by spinal cord injury or neurological disorders.

Causes

  • Spinal cord injuries.
  • Stroke or brain injury.
  • Diseases like multiple sclerosis.

Symptoms

  • Loss of movement in arms and legs.
  • Difficulty breathing in severe cases.
  • Loss of bladder and bowel control.

Treatment

Treatment includes physical therapy, surgery, assistive devices, and long-term rehabilitation.

Prevention

  • Wear protective gear to avoid spinal injuries.
  • Control risk factors for stroke.
  • Early treatment of neurological conditions.
