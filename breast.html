Breast Cancer

Overview

Breast cancer is a malignant tumor that develops in breast tissue, most common in women.

Causes

Genetic mutations (BRCA1/BRCA2), hormonal factors, lifestyle risks.

Symptoms

  • Lump in breast
  • Changes in breast shape
  • Nipple discharge
  • Breast pain

Treatment

Surgery, chemotherapy, radiation, hormone therapy, targeted drugs.

Prevention

Regular screening, healthy lifestyle, genetic testing for high-risk women.
