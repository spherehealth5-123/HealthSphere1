Retinoblastoma

Overview

Retinoblastoma is a rare type of eye cancer that develops in the retina, most often in young children.

Causes

  • Mutations in the RB1 gene
  • Inherited or spontaneous genetic changes

Symptoms

  • White reflection in pupil
  • Eye redness or swelling
  • Crossed eyes (strabismus)

Treatment

Options include chemotherapy, laser therapy, radiation, or surgery depending on stage.

Prevention

Not preventable, but genetic testing and early screening reduce risk.
