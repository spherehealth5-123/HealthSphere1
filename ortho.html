Orthostatic Tremor

Overview

Orthostatic Tremor is a rare movement disorder characterized by rapid muscle contractions in the legs when standing still.

Causes

The exact cause is unknown, but it is linked to neurological dysfunction in the brainstem or cerebellum.

Symptoms

  • Unsteadiness when standing
  • Leg tremors relieved by walking or sitting
  • Difficulty standing for long periods
  • Anxiety due to imbalance

Treatment

Managed with medications (clonazepam, gabapentin), physical therapy, and mobility aids.

Prevention

No known prevention; early diagnosis and lifestyle modifications help improve quality of life.
