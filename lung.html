Lung Cancer

Overview

Lung Cancer is one of the most common and deadly cancers worldwide, starting in the lungs.

Causes

Mainly caused by smoking, also linked to secondhand smoke, pollution, and genetics.

Symptoms

  • Persistent cough
  • Chest pain
  • Coughing blood
  • Weight loss

Treatment

Treatments include surgery, chemotherapy, radiation, and targeted drugs.

Prevention

Quit smoking, avoid carcinogens, and adopt healthy habits.
