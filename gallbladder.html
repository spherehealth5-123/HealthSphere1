Gallbladder Cancer

Overview

Gallbladder cancer is a rare but aggressive cancer that starts in the gallbladder.

Causes

Chronic gallstones, inflammation, and certain genetic mutations.

Symptoms

  • Abdominal pain
  • Jaundice
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Unexplained weight loss

Treatment

Surgical removal, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and palliative care.

Prevention

Maintaining a healthy weight and managing gallstones promptly.
