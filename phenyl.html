Phenylketonuria (PKU)

Overview

PKU is a rare inherited disorder that causes a buildup of the amino acid phenylalanine in the body.

Causes

  • Genetic mutation in the PAH gene
  • Lack of enzyme to break down phenylalanine

Symptoms

  • Intellectual disability
  • Seizures
  • Behavioral problems
  • Skin rashes

Treatment

  • Special low-phenylalanine diet
  • Use of medical formula for infants
  • Medications like sapropterin

Prevention

  • Newborn screening for early detection
  • Strict lifelong dietary management
