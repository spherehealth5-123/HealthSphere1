Diamond-Blackfan Anemia

Overview

Diamond-Blackfan anemia (DBA) is a rare blood disorder where the bone marrow fails to produce enough red blood cells.

Causes

Genetic mutations affecting ribosome function.

Symptoms

  • Severe anemia
  • Pale skin
  • Fatigue
  • Slow growth

Treatment

Blood transfusions, corticosteroids, and bone marrow transplant.

Prevention

No prevention; genetic testing for families at risk is recommended.
