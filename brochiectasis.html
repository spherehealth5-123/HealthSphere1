Bronchiectasis

Overview

Bronchiectasis is a chronic condition where the bronchial tubes are permanently widened and damaged.

Causes

Often due to repeated lung infections or underlying conditions like cystic fibrosis.

Symptoms

  • Chronic cough
  • Thick sputum
  • Shortness of breath
  • Frequent chest infections

Treatment

Antibiotics, chest physiotherapy, and in severe cases surgery.

Prevention

Vaccination, prompt treatment of infections, and avoiding smoking.
