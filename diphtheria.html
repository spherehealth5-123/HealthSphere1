Diphtheria

Overview

Diphtheria is a serious bacterial infection affecting the mucous membranes of the throat and nose.

Causes

Caused by Corynebacterium diphtheriae, spread through respiratory droplets.

Symptoms

  • Sore throat
  • Thick gray coating in throat
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Swollen glands

Treatment

Antitoxin to neutralize toxins and antibiotics like erythromycin or penicillin.

Prevention

DTaP/Tdap vaccination and maintaining good hygiene.
