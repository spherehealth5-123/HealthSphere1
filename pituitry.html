Pituitary Cancer

Overview

Pituitary cancer is a rare tumor that forms in the pituitary gland, affecting hormone production.

Causes

  • Genetic predisposition
  • Hormonal imbalances
  • Unknown factors in most cases

Symptoms

  • Vision problems
  • Headaches
  • Hormonal changes
  • Fatigue

Treatment

  • Surgery to remove tumor
  • Radiation therapy
  • Hormone replacement therapy

Prevention

  • No known prevention
  • Regular checkups for at-risk individuals
