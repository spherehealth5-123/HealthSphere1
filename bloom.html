Bloom Syndrome

Overview

Bloom syndrome is a rare genetic disorder causing growth deficiency and increased cancer risk.

Causes

Caused by mutations in the BLM gene.

Symptoms

  • Short stature
  • Skin sensitivity to sunlight
  • Frequent infections
  • High cancer risk

Treatment

No cure. Supportive care, regular cancer screening, and avoiding UV exposure.

Prevention

Genetic counseling for families with history of the disorder.
