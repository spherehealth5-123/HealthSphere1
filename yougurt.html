Yogurt Intolerance

Overview

A digestive intolerance where the body reacts poorly to yogurt or dairy products due to lactose or protein sensitivity.

Causes

  • Lactose intolerance.
  • Milk protein allergy.

Symptoms

  • Abdominal bloating, cramps, and diarrhea.
  • Nausea or vomiting.
  • Skin reactions in milk protein allergy cases.

Treatment

  • Avoid yogurt and other dairy products.
  • Lactose-free alternatives.
  • Medication to reduce symptoms, if necessary.

Prevention

  • Identify lactose or protein sensitivity and avoid triggers.
