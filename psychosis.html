Psychosis

Overview

Psychosis is a mental health condition where a person loses contact with reality, experiencing delusions or hallucinations.

Causes

  • Schizophrenia
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Substance abuse
  • Brain injury or trauma

Symptoms

  • Hallucinations
  • Delusions
  • Disorganized thinking
  • Withdrawal from social activities

Treatment

  • Antipsychotic medications
  • Therapy and counseling
  • Supportive care

Prevention

  • Early treatment of mental health conditions
  • Avoid drugs and alcohol
  • Stress management techniques
