Dengue Fever

Overview

Dengue fever is a mosquito-borne viral infection common in tropical regions.

Causes

Caused by the dengue virus, transmitted by Aedes aegypti mosquitoes.

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Severe headache
  • Muscle and joint pain
  • Rash and bleeding gums

Treatment

No specific cure. Supportive care includes hydration, fever control, and pain management.

Prevention

Mosquito control, protective clothing, insect repellents, and vaccination in some regions.
