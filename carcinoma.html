Carcinoma

Overview

Carcinoma is a type of cancer that starts in the epithelial cells that cover the body’s surfaces and internal organs.

Causes

DNA mutations caused by smoking, UV radiation, carcinogens, or hereditary factors.

Symptoms

  • Abnormal lump or growth
  • Skin lesions or sores
  • Unexplained bleeding
  • Persistent pain

Treatment

Surgery, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, targeted therapy, and immunotherapy.

Prevention

Avoid smoking, limit UV exposure, maintain a healthy lifestyle, and undergo cancer screening.
