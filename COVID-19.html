COVID-19

Overview

COVID-19 is a respiratory illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus, first identified in 2019.

Causes

Caused by infection with the novel coronavirus, spread through respiratory droplets.

Symptoms

  • Fever and cough
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue and muscle pain

Treatment

Supportive care, antiviral drugs, oxygen therapy in severe cases.

Prevention

Vaccination, mask use, hand hygiene, and physical distancing.
