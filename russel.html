Russell-Silver Syndrome

Overview

Russell-Silver Syndrome is a rare genetic disorder that causes growth delays before and after birth, leading to short stature and distinct facial features.

Causes

  • Genetic abnormalities involving chromosome 7 or 11
  • Imprinting defects

Symptoms

  • Poor growth and low birth weight
  • Asymmetry of body parts
  • Feeding difficulties and hypoglycemia

Treatment

Growth hormone therapy, nutritional support, and orthopedic care.

Prevention

Not preventable. Genetic counseling may help families at risk.
