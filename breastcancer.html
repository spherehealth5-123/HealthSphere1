Breast Cancer

HealthSphere Approach

People with breast cancer who seek care at HealthSphere will find dedicated breast cancer specialists who provide expert, personalized care using the latest treatment innovations and technology.

Your HealthSphere Care Team

Breast health specialists in the HealthSphere Breast Clinic coordinate care for people with breast cancer. These doctors focus solely on breast cancer and other conditions. They will meet with you to understand your needs and arrange the necessary tests and treatments, often within days at one facility.

  • Internal medicine specialists focused on breast conditions
  • Breast cancer surgeons
  • Medical oncologists
  • Pathologists
  • Plastic surgeons
  • Physical therapists
  • Radiation oncologists
  • Radiologists

Advanced Diagnosis and Treatment

HealthSphere doctors have access to the latest technology and clinical trials investigating new medical innovations. Your care team incorporates the newest evidence into your treatment plan.

Highly trained staff: Imaging is performed by radiologists who specialize in breast imaging, including those with advanced training.

Specialized screening: Custom screening programs for people with dense breast tissue and high-risk patients using supplemental techniques.

Latest technology: Digital mammogram, 3D mammogram (tomosynthesis), MRI, and molecular breast imaging (MBI).

Ongoing research: Studies on new diagnostic technologies like contrast-enhanced mammography and advanced MRI methods.
