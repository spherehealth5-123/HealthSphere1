Generalized Dystonia

Overview

Generalized dystonia is a neurological movement disorder that causes involuntary muscle contractions affecting multiple areas of the body.

Causes

Genetic mutations, brain injuries, or side effects of medications.

Symptoms

  • Muscle spasms
  • Abnormal postures
  • Painful movements
  • Difficulty walking

Treatment

Botulinum toxin injections, deep brain stimulation, physical therapy, and medications.

Prevention

No guaranteed prevention; early diagnosis and treatment can help manage symptoms.
