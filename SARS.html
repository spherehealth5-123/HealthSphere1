Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS)

Overview

SARS is a contagious and potentially life-threatening respiratory illness caused by the SARS coronavirus (SARS-CoV).

Causes

  • Infection with SARS-CoV virus
  • Spread through respiratory droplets

Symptoms

  • High fever and chills
  • Cough and shortness of breath
  • Muscle pain and fatigue

Treatment

No specific cure; treatment includes supportive care such as oxygen therapy, antivirals, and mechanical ventilation in severe cases.

Prevention

Good hygiene, wearing masks, avoiding close contact with infected individuals, and travel restrictions during outbreaks.
