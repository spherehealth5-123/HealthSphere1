Sezary Syndrome

Overview

Sezary Syndrome is a rare type of T-cell lymphoma that affects the skin, blood, and lymph nodes.

Causes

  • Abnormal growth of T-cells
  • Exact cause unknown

Symptoms

  • Red, itchy rash covering large areas of skin
  • Thickened skin and enlarged lymph nodes
  • Fatigue and weight loss

Treatment

Chemotherapy, immunotherapy, phototherapy, and stem cell transplantation.

Prevention

No known prevention; early diagnosis improves outcomes.
