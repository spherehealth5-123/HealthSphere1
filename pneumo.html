Pneumothorax

Overview

Pneumothorax, also known as a collapsed lung, occurs when air leaks into the space between the lung and chest wall.

Causes

  • Chest injury
  • Underlying lung disease
  • Ruptured air blisters
  • Mechanical ventilation

Symptoms

  • Sudden chest pain
  • Shortness of breath
  • Rapid heart rate
  • Fatigue

Treatment

  • Observation for small cases
  • Needle aspiration or chest tube insertion
  • Surgery for recurrent cases

Prevention

  • Avoid smoking
  • Manage lung diseases properly
  • Prevent chest injuries
