Xerostomia

Overview

Xerostomia is the medical term for dry mouth due to reduced or absent saliva production.

Causes

  • Medications (antihistamines, antidepressants).
  • Radiation therapy to the head and neck.
  • Autoimmune disorders like Sjögren's syndrome.

Symptoms

  • Dry, sticky feeling in the mouth.
  • Difficulty swallowing or speaking.
  • Increased dental decay.

Treatment

  • Artificial saliva substitutes.
  • Good oral hygiene.
  • Medication adjustment if possible.

Prevention

  • Stay hydrated.
  • Avoid alcohol and tobacco.
  • Regular dental check-ups.
