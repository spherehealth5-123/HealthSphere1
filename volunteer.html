General Volunteer Overview

Becoming a HealthSphere volunteer is a way to make a real difference, while meeting interesting peopleand occasionally even enjoying a free lunch! HealthSphere volunteers
range from high school students, to college students, to career volunteers to retired adults. They hail from the community or outside of the community. They volunteer
on weekdays, weekends and evenings—in offices, on patient units, and at health fairs.This is truly volunteering your way!
    If you are 16 years of age or older, please consider becoming a HealthSphere volunteer. To receive an application via email or to discuss opportunities for volunteering at HealthSphere 
Chicago (Mount Sinai Hospital, Schwab Rehabilitation, HealthSphere Children’s Hospital and/or HealthSphere Community Institute) contact Tondalaya Shepard.
