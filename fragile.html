Fragile X Syndrome

Overview

Fragile X syndrome is a genetic condition causing intellectual disability, behavioral issues, and distinctive physical features.

Causes

Mutation in the FMR1 gene on the X chromosome.

Symptoms

  • Developmental delays
  • Learning disabilities
  • Hyperactivity and anxiety
  • Distinct facial features

Treatment

Special education, behavioral therapy, speech therapy, and medications for symptoms.

Prevention

Genetic counseling for families with Fragile X history.
