Uterine Cancer

Overview

Uterine cancer begins in the lining of the uterus, also called endometrial cancer.

Causes

  • Hormonal imbalance
  • Obesity
  • Genetic predisposition

Symptoms

  • Abnormal vaginal bleeding
  • Pelvic pain
  • Pain during intercourse

Treatment

Surgery (hysterectomy), radiation, chemotherapy, hormone therapy.

Prevention

Maintaining healthy weight, controlling hormones, and regular checkups.
