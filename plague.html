Plague

Overview

Plague is a serious infectious disease caused by the bacterium Yersinia pestis. It is historically known as the Black Death.

Causes

  • Spread through bites from infected fleas
  • Contact with infected animals
  • Human-to-human transmission in pneumonic plague

Symptoms

  • Swollen lymph nodes (buboes)
  • Fever and chills
  • Weakness
  • Shortness of breath (in pneumonic plague)

Treatment

  • Antibiotics like streptomycin, gentamicin
  • Hospital isolation for pneumonic plague

Prevention

  • Avoid contact with rodents
  • Use insect repellents
  • Prompt treatment of suspected cases
