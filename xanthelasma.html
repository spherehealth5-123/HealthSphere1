Xanthelasma

Overview

Xanthelasma are yellowish deposits of cholesterol under the skin, commonly around the eyelids.

Causes

  • High cholesterol levels in the blood.
  • Genetic predisposition.

Symptoms

  • Yellowish, soft plaques on eyelids.
  • Usually painless and slow-growing.

Treatment

  • Laser therapy or surgical removal.
  • Medications to manage cholesterol levels.

Prevention

  • Maintain healthy cholesterol levels.
  • Regular medical checkups for lipid disorders.
