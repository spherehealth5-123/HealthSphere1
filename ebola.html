Ebola Virus Disease

Overview

Ebola virus disease is a severe and often fatal illness caused by the Ebola virus.

Causes

Spread through direct contact with blood, secretions, or bodily fluids of infected people or animals.

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Severe weakness
  • Bleeding and bruising
  • Organ failure

Treatment

Supportive care (rehydration, oxygen, and medication). Experimental antivirals and vaccines are available.

Prevention

Infection control, protective equipment, avoiding contact with infected animals, and vaccination in outbreak regions.
