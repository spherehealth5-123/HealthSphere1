Brain Cancer

Overview

Brain cancer refers to malignant tumors that grow within the brain tissue.

Causes

Caused by genetic mutations, radiation exposure, or family history.

Symptoms

  • Headaches
  • Seizures
  • Memory problems
  • Vision or speech issues

Treatment

Treatment may include surgery, chemotherapy, radiation, or targeted therapy.

Prevention

Limiting radiation exposure and healthy lifestyle may reduce risks.
