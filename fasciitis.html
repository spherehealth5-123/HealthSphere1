Fasciitis

Overview

Fasciitis is inflammation of the fascia, commonly the plantar fascia in the foot.

Causes

Overuse, poor footwear, obesity, or infections (necrotizing fasciitis).

Symptoms

  • Severe pain
  • Swelling
  • Redness
  • Tenderness

Treatment

Rest, anti-inflammatory medications, physical therapy, and surgery in severe cases.

Prevention

Wear proper footwear, maintain a healthy weight, and avoid prolonged strain.
