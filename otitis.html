Otitis Media

Overview

Otitis Media is an infection or inflammation of the middle ear, commonly affecting children.

Causes

Often caused by bacteria or viruses following a respiratory infection or allergies that block the Eustachian tube.

Symptoms

  • Ear pain
  • Hearing loss
  • Fever
  • Fluid drainage from the ear
  • Irritability in children

Treatment

May include antibiotics, pain relievers, or surgical ear tubes in chronic cases.

Prevention

Prevented by vaccinations, avoiding smoke exposure, and managing allergies.
