Focal Dystonia

Overview

Focal dystonia is a neurological condition causing involuntary muscle contractions in a specific part of the body.

Causes

Genetics, repetitive motion injuries, or nerve dysfunction.

Symptoms

  • Uncontrolled muscle contractions
  • Twitching or spasms
  • Pain and discomfort
  • Difficulty with fine motor tasks

Treatment

Botulinum toxin injections, physical therapy, medications, and stress management.

Prevention

Avoid repetitive strain and manage stress effectively.
