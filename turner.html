Turner Syndrome

Overview

Turner Syndrome is a chromosomal disorder in females where one X chromosome is missing or partially missing.

Causes

  • Missing/altered X chromosome
  • Not inherited, occurs randomly

Symptoms

  • Short stature
  • Delayed puberty
  • Infertility and heart defects

Treatment

Growth hormone therapy, estrogen replacement therapy, and fertility treatments.

Prevention

No prevention, but genetic counseling can provide guidance for families.
