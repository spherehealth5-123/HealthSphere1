Epilepsy

Overview

Epilepsy is a neurological disorder marked by recurrent, unprovoked seizures due to abnormal electrical activity in the brain.

Causes

Genetics, brain injury, infections, developmental disorders, or unknown factors.

Symptoms

  • Seizures
  • Temporary confusion
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Staring spells

Treatment

Anti-seizure medications, surgery, nerve stimulation, and dietary therapy.

Prevention

Prevent head injuries, treat infections promptly, and avoid seizure triggers.
