Progeria

Overview

Progeria is a rare genetic disorder that causes children to age rapidly, leading to early health complications.

Causes

  • Mutation in the LMNA gene
  • Abnormal production of the protein progerin

Symptoms

  • Growth delays
  • Baldness
  • Aged-looking skin
  • Heart disease in childhood

Treatment

  • No cure, but supportive care helps
  • Medications to reduce heart complications
  • Physical therapy for mobility

Prevention

  • No known prevention
  • Genetic counseling for families at risk
