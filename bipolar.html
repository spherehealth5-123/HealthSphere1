Bipolar Disorder

Overview

Bipolar disorder is a mental health condition with extreme mood swings including mania and depression.

Causes

Combination of genetic, environmental, and brain chemistry factors.

Symptoms

  • Episodes of mania
  • Episodes of depression
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Impulsive behavior

Treatment

Mood stabilizers, psychotherapy, lifestyle management.

Prevention

No cure, but early treatment helps prevent severe episodes.
