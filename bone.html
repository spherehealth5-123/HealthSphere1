Bone Marrow Transplants

Hospital Approach

At HealthSphere, our approach is built around patient-centered care. We specialize in autologous (self-cell), allogeneic (donor-cell), and cord blood transplants. Every patient is given a personalized treatment plan that considers medical condition, age, lifestyle, and emotional needs. We also provide pre-transplant counseling, nutritional support, and post-transplant follow-ups to ensure the best outcomes.

Our Team

Our transplant team includes highly experienced hematologists, oncologists, immunologists, and pathologists who collaborate closely to ensure safe and effective procedures. Alongside them, psychologists, dietitians, social workers, and transplant coordinators guide patients and families at every stage. This multi-disciplinary model ensures patients receive not only medical expertise but also psychological and emotional care throughout their journey.

The Clinic Experience

Patients benefit from advanced facilities, dedicated transplant units, and round-the-clock nursing support. Over the years, we have successfully performed thousands of bone marrow transplants, combining technology with compassion. Our hospital experience is designed to be stress-free, with family support systems, recovery lounges, and wellness programs to aid rehabilitation and recovery after treatment.

Research & Innovation

We constantly work on improving transplant outcomes through innovation. Our ongoing research includes haploidentical transplants (using partially matched donors), reduced-intensity chemotherapy to make procedures safer for older patients, and the use of cord blood stem cells to expand treatment options. We are also pioneering new medications that minimize graft-versus-host disease (GVHD) and improve patient survival rates.

Patient Stories

Patients often share how the transplant not only saved their lives but also gave them a renewed sense of hope. Many families appreciate the continuous support during and after the procedure, including guidance for managing recovery at home. These stories inspire others to pursue treatment confidently and highlight the importance of trust, communication, and empathy in healthcare.

Eligibility

Bone marrow transplants are considered for patients with leukemia, lymphoma, multiple myeloma, aplastic anemia, immune deficiencies, and other blood disorders. Before recommending a transplant, our team evaluates medical history, test results, organ health, and overall readiness to undergo the procedure. Patients who may not qualify for a transplant are offered advanced alternatives tailored to their needs.

Costs & Insurance

We provide transparent cost estimates and financial counseling before starting treatment. Our hospital is partnered with leading insurance providers to make the process smoother. In cases where insurance does not cover all expenses, our financial assistance team helps patients explore options like grants, sponsorships, and payment plans. This ensures finances never become a barrier to life-saving treatment.

Clinical Trials

We actively participate in global clinical trials that test innovative transplant techniques and supportive therapies. Patients may be eligible to join these studies, gaining access to the latest treatments before they become widely available. Our focus is to continuously advance medical science while giving patients the best chance at recovery with cutting-edge therapies.
