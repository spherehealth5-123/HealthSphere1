Soft Tissue Sarcoma

Overview

Soft Tissue Sarcoma is a group of cancers that begin in tissues such as muscles, fat, blood vessels, and nerves.

Causes

  • Genetic mutations
  • Radiation exposure
  • Certain inherited syndromes

Symptoms

  • Painless lump or swelling
  • Pain if tumor presses on nerves or muscles
  • Reduced mobility

Treatment

Surgery, radiation therapy, and chemotherapy depending on stage and location.

Prevention

Not fully preventable, but avoiding radiation exposure and genetic counseling may help.
