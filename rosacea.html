Rosacea

Overview

Rosacea is a chronic skin condition that causes redness, visible blood vessels, and sometimes pimples on the face.

Causes

  • Genetic predisposition
  • Immune system overreaction
  • Environmental triggers (heat, alcohol, stress)

Symptoms

  • Facial redness and flushing
  • Visible blood vessels
  • Thickened skin in severe cases

Treatment

Topical creams, oral antibiotics, and laser therapy for visible vessels.

Prevention

Avoid known triggers, use sunscreen, and maintain a gentle skincare routine.
