Upper Respiratory Tract Infection (URTI)

Overview

URTIs are common viral or bacterial infections affecting the nose, throat, and sinuses.

Causes

  • Viruses (cold, influenza)
  • Bacteria (strep throat)
  • Exposure to infected individuals

Symptoms

  • Sore throat
  • Cough and congestion
  • Runny nose
  • Fever

Treatment

Rest, hydration, over-the-counter medicines, antibiotics if bacterial.

Prevention

Hand hygiene, masks, vaccinations (flu), and avoiding sick contacts.
