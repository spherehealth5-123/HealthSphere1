Spinal Cord Injury

Overview

A Spinal Cord Injury results from trauma or disease affecting the spinal cord, often leading to loss of movement and sensation.

Causes

  • Accidents and falls
  • Sports injuries
  • Disease-related damage (tumors, infections)

Symptoms

  • Loss of movement and sensation
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Pain or pressure in the back/neck

Treatment

Emergency care, surgery, rehabilitation therapy, and assistive devices.

Prevention

Wearing protective gear, safe driving, fall prevention, and maintaining strong bone health.
