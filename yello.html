Yellow Nail Syndrome

Overview

A rare disorder characterized by yellow nails, respiratory problems, and lymphedema (fluid swelling).

Causes

  • Exact cause unknown.
  • May involve lymphatic drainage problems.

Symptoms

  • Thickened, yellow nails that grow slowly.
  • Swelling in arms and legs (lymphedema).
  • Chronic respiratory infections or bronchitis.

Treatment

  • Address respiratory infections with medications.
  • Supportive care for lymphedema.

Prevention

  • No known prevention; regular checkups and monitoring recommended.
