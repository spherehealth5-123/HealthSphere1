Down Syndrome

Overview

Down syndrome is a genetic disorder caused by an extra chromosome 21.

Causes

Trisomy 21 due to abnormal cell division during development.

Symptoms

  • Distinct facial features
  • Intellectual disability
  • Delayed development
  • Heart defects

Treatment

No cure. Supportive therapies include speech, occupational, and physical therapy.

Prevention

No prevention, but genetic counseling and prenatal screening help with early detection.
