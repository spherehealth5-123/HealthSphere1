Sleep Apnea

Overview

Sleep Apnea is a sleep disorder where breathing repeatedly stops and starts during sleep.

Causes

  • Obstructive sleep apnea: airway blockage
  • Central sleep apnea: brain not sending signals
  • Complex sleep apnea: combination of both

Symptoms

  • Loud snoring
  • Episodes of stopped breathing
  • Daytime fatigue

Treatment

CPAP machine, weight management, surgery, and lifestyle changes.

Prevention

Maintaining healthy weight, avoiding alcohol, quitting smoking, and sleeping on your side.
