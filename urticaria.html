Urticaria (Hives)

Overview

Urticaria, or hives, is a skin condition marked by itchy, raised welts often caused by allergic reactions.

Causes

  • Allergic reactions (food, drugs, insect bites)
  • Infections
  • Stress or temperature changes

Symptoms

  • Itchy red welts
  • Swelling of lips/eyelids (angioedema)

Treatment

Antihistamines, corticosteroids, avoiding triggers.

Prevention

Identifying and avoiding allergens, stress management.
