Ringworm

Overview

Ringworm is a fungal skin infection that causes red, circular rashes. Despite the name, it is not caused by worms.

Causes

  • Dermatophyte fungi
  • Spread through direct skin contact or contaminated items

Symptoms

  • Itchy, ring-shaped rash
  • Red, scaly patches
  • Brittle or discolored nails (in nail infection)

Treatment

Antifungal creams, ointments, and oral medications for severe cases.

Prevention

  • Maintain good hygiene
  • Avoid sharing towels or clothing
  • Keep skin clean and dry
