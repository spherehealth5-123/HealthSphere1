Vestibular Disorder

Overview

A group of conditions affecting the inner ear and balance system, leading to dizziness and unsteadiness.

Causes

  • Inner ear infections.
  • Head injury.
  • Aging or genetic disorders.

Symptoms

  • Vertigo (spinning sensation).
  • Loss of balance.
  • Blurred vision.
  • Nausea and vomiting.

Treatment

  • Vestibular rehabilitation therapy (VRT).
  • Medications for nausea and dizziness.
  • Surgery in rare cases.

Prevention

  • Protect head from injury.
  • Treat ear infections early.
  • Maintain healthy lifestyle.
