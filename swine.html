Swine Flu (H1N1)

Overview

Swine Flu is a respiratory disease caused by the H1N1 influenza virus, which spreads from person to person.

Causes

  • H1N1 influenza virus
  • Spread through coughing, sneezing, or touching contaminated surfaces

Symptoms

  • Fever and chills
  • Cough, sore throat, and runny nose
  • Body aches and fatigue

Treatment

Antiviral medications (oseltamivir, zanamivir), rest, hydration, and fever control with medications.

Prevention

Vaccination, good hygiene, and avoiding contact with infected individuals.
