Typhoid

Overview

Typhoid is a bacterial infection caused by Salmonella Typhi, usually spread through contaminated food and water.

Causes

  • Consumption of contaminated food or water
  • Poor sanitation and hygiene

Symptoms

  • High fever and weakness
  • Abdominal pain and constipation/diarrhea
  • Skin rash with rose-colored spots

Treatment

Antibiotics (ciprofloxacin, azithromycin), rehydration, and supportive care.

Prevention

Typhoid vaccination, safe drinking water, improved sanitation practices.
