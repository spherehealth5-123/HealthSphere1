Von Hippel-Lindau Syndrome

Overview

A rare inherited disorder that causes tumors and cysts in different parts of the body, particularly the brain, spine, kidneys, and eyes.

Causes

  • Mutation in the VHL gene.
  • Inherited in an autosomal dominant pattern.

Symptoms

  • Hemangioblastomas (tumors in brain and spinal cord)
  • Kidney cysts and cancer
  • Vision problems due to retinal tumors

Treatment

  • Regular screening for tumors.
  • Surgical removal of tumors when needed.
  • Targeted therapies for kidney cancer.

Prevention

  • Genetic counseling for affected families.
  • Routine monitoring to catch tumors early.
