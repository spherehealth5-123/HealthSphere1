Thymic Cancer

Overview

Thymic cancer is a rare malignancy that forms in the thymus gland located in the chest behind the sternum.

Causes

  • Exact cause unknown
  • May be linked to autoimmune diseases

Symptoms

  • Chest pain and cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Swelling of face/neck

Treatment

Surgical removal, chemotherapy, and radiation therapy.

Prevention

No known prevention; early diagnosis improves outcomes.
