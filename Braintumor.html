Bone Marrow Transplant

HealthSphere Approach

HealthSphere experts have extensive experience using bone marrow transplants to treat a variety of conditions, including cancers. We provide transplants for adults and children using their own cells (autologous transplant), cells from a donor (allogeneic transplant), or cells from umbilical cord blood (cord blood transplant).

Patients at HealthSphere receive comprehensive, compassionate, and personalized care from specialists dedicated to their needs. In addition, support groups and recovery programs are available to help patients and families through the treatment process.

The HealthSphere Experience

HealthSphere specialists have performed more than 16,000 bone marrow transplants since our program began. With decades of expertise, our care team ensures patients receive world-class treatment and support.

Advanced Diagnosis & Treatment

Patients at HealthSphere have access to the latest imaging and diagnostic tools. Our neuropathologists analyze thousands of tissue samples every year, including very rare tumor types, ensuring accurate diagnoses that guide effective treatment plans.

Our innovations include technologies like magnetic resonance elastography (MRE) that provide critical details for safe brain tumor surgeries. We also offer advanced surgical options such as:

  • Computer-assisted brain surgery
  • Intraoperative MRI
  • Awake brain surgery
  • Laser surgery
