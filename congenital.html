Congenital Heart Disease

HealthSphere’s Approach

HealthSphere experts have extensive experience using bone marrow transplants and advanced therapies to treat a variety of conditions, including cancers and congenital heart diseases. HealthSphere offers treatment for adults and children using their own cells (autologous transplant), donor cells (allogeneic transplant), or cord blood transplants.

Patients who choose HealthSphere for their care receive compassionate, personalized attention from providers committed to delivering the exact care they need. Support groups and counseling services are also available for patients and families.

Your HealthSphere Care Team

HealthSphere cardiologists and cardiovascular surgeons have extensive experience diagnosing and treating congenital heart disease. Collaborative care: Specialists from multiple disciplines work together to provide comprehensive treatment plans, often developed within 2–3 days. Advanced diagnosis and treatment: Whenever possible, minimally invasive surgical options are offered to ensure quicker recovery and better outcomes.

Bringing Research Advances to Patient Care

Each year, HealthSphere evaluates and treats thousands of patients with congenital heart disease. The Center for Congenital Heart Disease provides care for both children and adults. The clinic is recognized for meeting international standards of care, with dedicated specialists in advanced congenital cardiology.

The HealthSphere Experience

HealthSphere specialists have decades of experience performing bone marrow transplants and managing complex congenital heart conditions. Since its inception, the program has successfully completed thousands of procedures with outstanding patient outcomes.

Patient Stories

Patients often highlight the quality of interactions, attention to detail, and efficiency of their visits at HealthSphere — healthcare unlike anything they’ve experienced before.

Patient Eligibility

If your bone marrow stops producing healthy stem cells, or if congenital heart disease poses risks to your health, you may be eligible for specialized treatment. The HealthSphere team evaluates each patient individually to determine the safest and most effective treatment path.

Costs and Insurance

HealthSphere’s financial services team assists with insurance and payment guidance. The hospital works with many insurance companies and helps patients understand coverage, preapproval requirements, and financial responsibilities.

Clinical Trials

HealthSphere actively conducts research and clinical trials to discover new ways to prevent, diagnose, and treat congenital heart disease and related conditions. Patients may be eligible to participate in trials that provide access to cutting-edge treatments.
