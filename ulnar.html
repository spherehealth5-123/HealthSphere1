Ulnar-Mammary Syndrome

Overview

Ulnar-mammary syndrome is a rare genetic disorder affecting limb, gland, and hormonal development.

Causes

  • Mutations in the TBX3 gene

Symptoms

  • Abnormalities in forearm and hand
  • Underdeveloped mammary glands
  • Delayed puberty

Treatment

Hormone therapy, reconstructive surgery, and supportive care.

Prevention

Genetic counseling for affected families.
