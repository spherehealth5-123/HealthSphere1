Ulcerative Encephalitis

Overview

Ulcerative encephalitis is a rare inflammatory brain condition, usually caused by infection or autoimmune response.

Causes

  • Viral infections (herpes simplex, measles)
  • Autoimmune disorders

Symptoms

  • Fever and headache
  • Seizures
  • Confusion and memory loss

Treatment

Antivirals, corticosteroids, immunotherapy, and supportive care.

Prevention

Vaccination against viruses, and prompt treatment of infections.
