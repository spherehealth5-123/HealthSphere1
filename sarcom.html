Hospital Approach

The doctors at HealthSphere Comprehensive Cancer Center are respected for their expertise in diagnosing and treating sarcoma. At HealthSphere, you can expect compassionate, comprehensive care that's focused on your individual needs.

Your HealthSphere Care Team

At HealthSphere, specialists in sarcoma include adult and pediatric medical oncologists, radiation oncologists, orthopedic and general surgeons, and pathologists. They work as a multidisciplinary team to care for people with sarcoma. Other healthcare professionals are included as needed, such as specialists in physical medicine and rehabilitation.

A full range of treatment options:

  • Surgery, including innovations designed to preserve function as much as possible, such as rotationplasty and prosthetics.
  • Systemic therapies, including chemotherapy, targeted therapy and immunotherapy medicines.
  • Radiation therapy, including innovative treatments such as intraoperative radiation therapy (IORT) and proton therapy.
  • Access to clinical trials.
