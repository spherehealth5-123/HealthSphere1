Emphysema

Overview

Emphysema is a chronic lung condition where the air sacs (alveoli) are damaged, leading to shortness of breath.

Causes

Long-term smoking, air pollution, occupational exposure, and genetic factors.

Symptoms

  • Shortness of breath
  • Chronic cough
  • Wheezing
  • Fatigue

Treatment

Medications (bronchodilators, steroids), pulmonary rehabilitation, oxygen therapy, and surgery in severe cases.

Prevention

Avoid smoking, reduce exposure to pollutants, and maintain lung health.
