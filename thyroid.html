Follicular Thyroid Cancer

Overview

Follicular thyroid cancer is a type of thyroid cancer that arises from follicular cells responsible for producing thyroid hormones.

Causes

Genetic mutations, radiation exposure, and family history.

Symptoms

  • Lump in the neck
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Hoarseness
  • Swollen lymph nodes

Treatment

Surgery (thyroidectomy), radioactive iodine therapy, and thyroid hormone therapy.

Prevention

Regular checkups and avoiding unnecessary radiation exposure.
