Worm Infestations

Overview

Worm infestations occur when parasitic worms infect the intestines or other parts of the body, causing malnutrition and illness.

Causes

  • Contaminated food and water.
  • Poor hygiene and sanitation.
  • Walking barefoot on contaminated soil.

Symptoms

  • Stomach pain, nausea, diarrhea.
  • Weight loss and fatigue.
  • Visible worms in stool.

Treatment

  • Anti-parasitic medications (albendazole, mebendazole).
  • Improved nutrition and hydration.

Prevention

  • Wash hands before eating.
  • Cook food properly and drink clean water.
  • Wear footwear in contaminated areas.
