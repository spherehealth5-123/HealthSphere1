Becker Muscular Dystrophy

Overview

Becker muscular dystrophy is a genetic disorder affecting muscles, leading to progressive weakness.

Causes

Caused by mutations in the dystrophin gene, inherited in an X-linked recessive pattern.

Symptoms

  • Muscle weakness
  • Difficulty walking
  • Heart problems
  • Fatigue

Treatment

No cure. Physical therapy, steroids, and cardiac care help manage symptoms.

Prevention

Genetic counseling for at-risk families.
