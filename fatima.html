Dr. Fatima

Orthopedic Specialist

Biography

Dr. Fatima is an orthopedic surgeon specializing in bone and joint surgeries. She is passionate about restoring mobility and ensuring a pain-free life for her patients.

Qualification

  • MS (Orthopedic Surgery)
  • Fellowship in Joint Replacement – UK

Experience

Dr. Fatima has more than 12 years of experience in treating fractures, arthritis, and sports injuries with both surgical and non-surgical techniques.
