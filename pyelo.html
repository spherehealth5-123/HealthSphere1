Pyelonephritis

Overview

Pyelonephritis is a type of urinary tract infection (UTI) that reaches the kidneys, causing inflammation and pain.

Causes

  • Bacterial infection (commonly E. coli)
  • Blockages in urinary tract
  • Weakened immune system

Symptoms

  • Back or flank pain
  • Fever and chills
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Frequent urination

Treatment

  • Antibiotics
  • Pain relievers
  • Hospitalization in severe cases

Prevention

  • Good personal hygiene
  • Stay hydrated
  • Treat UTIs promptly
