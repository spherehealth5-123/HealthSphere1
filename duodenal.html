Duodenal Cancer

Overview

Duodenal cancer is a rare type of cancer that develops in the first part of the small intestine.

Causes

Genetic mutations, Crohn’s disease, celiac disease, and family history of gastrointestinal cancers.

Symptoms

  • Abdominal pain
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Blood in stool
  • Unexplained weight loss

Treatment

Surgical removal (Whipple procedure), chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and targeted therapy.

Prevention

Healthy diet, avoiding smoking/alcohol, and early screening for high-risk individuals.
