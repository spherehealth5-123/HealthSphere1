Usher Syndrome

Overview

Usher syndrome is a rare genetic condition that causes both hearing loss and vision impairment due to retinitis pigmentosa.

Causes

  • Inherited genetic mutations

Symptoms

  • Hearing loss
  • Night blindness
  • Loss of peripheral vision

Treatment

No cure; supportive therapies include hearing aids, cochlear implants, and vision rehabilitation.

Prevention

Genetic counseling for at-risk families.
