Epidermolysis Bullosa

Overview

Epidermolysis bullosa (EB) is a rare genetic condition causing fragile skin that blisters easily.

Causes

Mutations in genes responsible for skin integrity and collagen formation.

Symptoms

  • Skin blisters from minor injuries
  • Painful wounds
  • Thickened skin on palms and soles
  • Scarring and deformities

Treatment

No cure. Wound care, pain management, nutritional support, and experimental therapies.

Prevention

Prevent skin trauma, genetic testing, and supportive care to reduce complications.
