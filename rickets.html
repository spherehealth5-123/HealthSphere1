Rickets

Overview

Rickets is a bone disorder in children caused by prolonged vitamin D deficiency, leading to soft and weak bones.

Causes

  • Lack of vitamin D
  • Inadequate calcium intake
  • Genetic factors in rare cases

Symptoms

  • Bone pain and tenderness
  • Delayed growth
  • Bow-legged posture

Treatment

Vitamin D and calcium supplements, sunlight exposure, and in severe cases, corrective surgery.

Prevention

Ensure sufficient sunlight exposure, proper nutrition, and supplementation if needed.
