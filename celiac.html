Celiac Disease

Overview

Celiac disease is an autoimmune disorder where ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine.

Causes

Triggered by gluten consumption in genetically predisposed individuals.

Symptoms

  • Chronic diarrhea
  • Weight loss
  • Abdominal bloating
  • Fatigue and anemia

Treatment

Strict lifelong gluten-free diet.

Prevention

No prevention, but early diagnosis and gluten avoidance prevent complications.
