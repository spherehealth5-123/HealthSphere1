Xerophthalmia

Overview

Xerophthalmia is a condition of dry eyes caused by vitamin A deficiency, leading to night blindness and eye damage.

Causes

  • Vitamin A deficiency.
  • Chronic malnutrition.

Symptoms

  • Night blindness.
  • Dryness of the eyes and conjunctiva.
  • Corneal ulcers in severe cases.

Treatment

  • Vitamin A supplementation.
  • Diet rich in vitamin A (carrots, leafy greens).

Prevention

  • Ensure sufficient dietary intake of vitamin A.
  • Public health programs in high-risk areas.
