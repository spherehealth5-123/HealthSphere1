Qazi-Markouizos Syndrome

Overview

Qazi-Markouizos Syndrome is a very rare inherited disorder characterized by developmental delay, facial anomalies, and metabolic issues.

Causes

  • Genetic mutation passed through families.
  • Errors in metabolic or developmental pathways.

Symptoms

  • Growth and developmental delays.
  • Distinctive facial features.
  • Possible neurological impairments.

Treatment

There is no specific cure. Supportive therapies like speech, occupational, and physiotherapy are provided along with medical monitoring.

Prevention

  • Genetic counseling for families with history of the syndrome.
  • Early diagnosis to plan supportive care.
