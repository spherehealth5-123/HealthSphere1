Cholera

Overview

Cholera is an acute diarrheal disease caused by ingestion of contaminated food or water.

Causes

Caused by the bacterium Vibrio cholerae.

Symptoms

  • Severe watery diarrhea
  • Vomiting
  • Rapid dehydration
  • Muscle cramps

Treatment

Oral rehydration salts, intravenous fluids, antibiotics in severe cases.

Prevention

Safe water, sanitation, hygiene, and vaccination.
