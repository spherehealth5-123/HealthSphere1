Brain Aneurysm

HealthSphere Approach

HealthSphere experts have extensive experience treating brain aneurysms and related vascular conditions. Our approach includes advanced imaging, minimally invasive procedures, and comprehensive post-care. Patients benefit from personalized treatment plans, emotional support, and access to multiple support groups.

Your HealthSphere Care Team

Our team includes neurologists, neurosurgeons, and neuroradiologists with vast experience in cerebrovascular diseases. The team approach allows rapid diagnosis and treatment planning, often within a few days, ensuring timely care for both ruptured and unruptured aneurysms.

Bringing Research Advances to Patient Care

Treatment Expertise: Techniques include coiling, stenting, and flow diverters for complex aneurysms.

Individualized Treatment: Each plan is based on aneurysm size, location, family history, and risk of rupture.

Diagnostic Tests: Advanced imaging such as MRI, CT angiography, and cerebral arteriography aid in precise diagnosis.

Research: HealthSphere specialists lead clinical trials to discover new diagnostic tools and treatments.
