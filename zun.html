Zunich-Kaye Syndrome

Overview

Zunich-Kaye Syndrome is a rare congenital disorder causing developmental delays and skeletal abnormalities.

Causes

  • Likely genetic mutations
  • Possible autosomal recessive inheritance

Symptoms

  • Delayed growth and development
  • Skeletal malformations
  • Mild intellectual disability

Treatment

  • Symptom management (physical therapy, orthopedic care)
  • Educational support for developmental delays

Prevention

  • Genetic counseling for families with congenital disorders
