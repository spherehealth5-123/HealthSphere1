Gastric Cancer

Overview

Gastric cancer, also called stomach cancer, begins in the lining of the stomach.

Causes

Helicobacter pylori infection, smoking, diet high in salty foods, and genetics.

Symptoms

  • Indigestion
  • Loss of appetite
  • Stomach pain
  • Blood in stool

Treatment

Surgery, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and targeted therapies.

Prevention

Healthy diet, avoiding smoking, and treating H. pylori infection.
