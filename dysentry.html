Dysentery

Overview

Dysentery is an intestinal infection causing severe diarrhea with blood and mucus.

Causes

Caused by bacteria (Shigella) or parasites (Entamoeba histolytica).

Symptoms

  • Bloody diarrhea
  • Severe abdominal pain
  • Fever
  • Dehydration

Treatment

Rehydration therapy, antibiotics, and antiparasitic medications.

Prevention

Safe drinking water, proper sanitation, and handwashing.
