Yeast Infection

Overview

A fungal infection caused by Candida species, commonly affecting genital areas, mouth, or skin folds.

Causes

  • Overgrowth of Candida fungus.
  • Antibiotic use disrupting normal flora.
  • Weakened immune system.

Symptoms

  • Itching and irritation in affected area.
  • Redness or rash.
  • Discharge (for vaginal yeast infections).

Treatment

  • Topical antifungal creams or ointments.
  • Oral antifungal medications in severe cases.

Prevention

  • Maintain good hygiene.
  • Wear breathable clothing.
  • Avoid unnecessary antibiotic use.
